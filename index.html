It's getting close but no worries, you'll do good! :)

Elevator pitch - One representative from each team will get 1 minute on the stage to quickly present what you have been doing (and which room you're in). You also have the possibility to show an image on the projector. Add it to your Github repository before 17.30(!).

Demo & Feedback - Two from each team will stay at your product to demo it, explain it and answer questions. Please make sure to write down any feedback, even if you're not interested in developing it further, someone else might! You will have time to check out the work of five other teams, and we will ring a bell when it's time to move on to the next team (about 8 minutes).

If you have any questions, just grab one of us staffers and we'll try to answer any questions you might have!
